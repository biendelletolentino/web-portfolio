The Simple Love Letter with Background Music is a website that transforms a basic message into a surprising digital reveal. With its vintage aesthetic and easy to read cursive font, the design ensures the reader's focus remains entirely on the words, creating the nostalgic feeling of a hand written note in a modern format.

GoPrime SSO

The GoPrime SSO system allows administrators to create user accounts directly within the platform. When a new employee or user is onboarded, an administrator registers their account by providing basic details such as name, email address, and department. Once the account is created, the administrator assigns a role to the user such as Admin, Manager, or Employee which determines what applications and features they can access across all connected systems.
